Slaghammers is a feminist welding collective, prioritising learning for women, trans and non-binary people. We have been running since 2016, learning together, teaching folks metalwork skills, and creating beautiful furniture, sculptures, art installations, theatre props and more out of metal!

Commissions

We are currently open for commissions. If you are looking to get something fabricated in metal, please get in touch.

Access

Our workshop is on the ground floor and is wheelchair accessible. There is a toilet in the workshop and an accessible toilet in an adjacent building a 2 minute walk away.

Please get in touch if you would like to discuss access options.
